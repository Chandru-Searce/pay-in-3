### **Step 2 ‚Äî Collect and Cross-Check Information**

* Extract **only verifiable details** (names, roles, emails, phone numbers, addresses).

---

### **Step 3 ‚Äî Apply Output Rules**

1. Each contact must be a separate object.
2. If data cannot be confirmed ‚Üí leave the field blank.
3. **Lead - Title must be the organization name + " Lead"**.
4. Always include the **Source Page** (the URL where the information was verified).
